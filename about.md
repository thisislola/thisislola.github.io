### Hello!

I'm a Digital Forensics student in Norway. This blog is some sort of open notebook to me, or a collection of documentation I'm always writing for myself, but that I think someone out there might find useful. 

I love following other's tutorials that I find online to practice and get better at things, but sometimes it is either assumed the reader is not a newbie or the guide is not updated, so I am hoping I can tackle both issues with this tiny blog.  

I focus on security, forensics, tools I use, tools I love and tools I am learning to use, plus other interesting things! Read, enjoy and if you have any comments, let me know :)
